About Milo


Drawing from his eclectic roots in the East Bay Area, Milo enjoys breathing life into the synthesis of dynamic user interfaces. Milo's educational background includes a Bachelors of Science Degree in Biology from Saint Marys College of California, as well as completion of UC-Berkeley Coding Bootcamp.

Having a diverse educational background and scope of technological interest, Milo would describe himself as a full-stack technological liaison. Having a drive for excellence coupled with a spark for intelligent design, Milo continually strives for the development of cutting edge digital landscapes. In Milo's perspective, there are no boundries to human creativity, except for those which the mind creates.

In his spare time, Milo enjoys spending time outdoors, skateboarding, cooking, spending time with friends, and listening to music. His most noteworthy culinary expertise lies in homemade pizza, a skill refined from throwing many pies and careful experimentation.
